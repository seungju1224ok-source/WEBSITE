Four Key Problems SAFETIQUE Addresses

The real issue is not that safety equipment does not exist. It is that people do not notice it, remember it, or access it quickly when it matters.

Interior Conflict

The red color and industrial appearance of fire extinguishers often clash with cafes, hotels, galleries, showrooms, and other carefully designed spaces.

Recognition Gap

When equipment is placed out of sight, customers and operators may not know where it is located, reducing immediate usability in an emergency.

Weak Safety Trust

Dusty, hidden, or neglected equipment can make a space feel poorly managed and weaken the safety trust customers have in the brand.

OUR SOLUTION

Not a Cover.
A Safety Object.

SAFETIQUE is designed to preserve emergency accessibility while turning safety equipment into a visual asset that belongs in the space.
